TECH Talks

We are honored to feature a lineup of Tech Talks at SUMMERCON CRCE that will showcase the latest research and vulnerability disclosures. Attendees can expect to be impressed by the depth of knowledge and expertise on display, particularly in areas such as threat hunting, IR, DFIR, smart contract security, reverse engineering, and web application security.

CXO Panel:

The CXO panel at the 2023 SummerCon CRCE will address two key topics: Digital Trust and How to get a job in Cybersecurity. Panelists will discuss challenges in establishing trust in the digital age, emerging technologies' impact, and the importance of transparency. They will also offer valuable insights on in-demand skills and qualifications, tips to stand out in the job market, and navigating cybersecurity career paths.

Job Fair

We are excited to announce that for the first time, we are bringing together Infosec organizations and talented resources through a combined job fair. Our goal is to bridge the gap between these two groups and provide a platform for them to connect and explore potential opportunities.
